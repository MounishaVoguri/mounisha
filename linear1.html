Linear Regression Predictor

What is Linear Regression?

Linear regression is a type of supervised machine-learning algorithm that learns from labeled datasets and maps the data points with the most optimized linear function. This function can then be used for prediction on new data.
Application: Salary prediction
The the salary of the employee is predicted on the bases of his/her working experiences.
X=number of years of experience
Y=predicted salary (in 10000)
